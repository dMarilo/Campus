# Campus

Universities are complex systems.

They are not just lists of students and courses, but living organizations made up of campuses, buildings, rooms, professors, academic years, classes, enrollments, and rules that vary from one institution to another.

Our goal is to build a **flexible, instance-based campus platform** that can model this reality without forcing every institution into the same rigid structure.
